• Sunday Worship

    Come and celebrate with us every Sunday morning as we praise Jesus in song, share His divine Word and break bread in remembrance of the Lord's Supper.

    For more information please contact us.

  • Wednesday Prayer

    We gather to speak and to listen to Jesus as one community under His Lordship. All are welcome to lift their hearts to him, aloud and in silent prayer. We can all call on the Lord’s Name, knowing that each prayer is lifted up to God by the assembled community of faith.

    For more information please contact us

  • Young Christian's Friday

    Remembering our creator in the days of our youth (Ecclesiastes 12:1), our young Christians meet on Friday evenings. This is a time to get to know each other and to share our journeys as we grow into Christian adulthood. Sometimes it’s just about good clean let-your-hair-down FUN!

    For more information please contact us
