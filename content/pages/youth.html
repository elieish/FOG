Young Christians' Friday
Not forgetting our Maker in our youth, we meet on Friday evenings. This is a time to get to know each other, to share each other's journeys be they spiritual or moral; sometimes it is just to have good old let-your-hair-down clean fun. Saturday Good News Club Suffer not the little children to come unto him. We focus on age-appropriate inputs on moral regeneration targeting children between 8 and 12 years of age. This helps our Christian family rise to the challenge of inculcating a strong Christian character and a love for Christ.
